Villa Sonnenblick

Enjoy your holidays in our beautiful holiday villa in Kötschach-Mauthen, Carinthia.

It is a beautiful spot in Carinthia, in southern Austria just over the Italian border. Just 10 hours from the Netherlands and Belgium is it an ideal vacation spot! In the winter there is always snow, and you can walk to the ski slopes, The summer it is pleasantly warm and sunny, and you can walk in the green mountains and valleys, villages nearby to visit, or just visits over the border Italy (Venice and Grado) or Slovenia.

In this fantastic location, we offer you our holiday villa. Equipped with all the comforts you will have a wonderful and undisturbed, carefree holiday experience here! The villa is located in a family resort overlooking the ski slope and ski lift. On 20 km is the Nassfeld ski area (free ski bus). Want to know more? Check out the photos, read more about the house and its surroundings, check prices and book your ideal vacation spot! You can always call or email us for more information! See you in Kötschach-Mauthen!
